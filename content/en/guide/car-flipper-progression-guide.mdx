It is vital to distinguish between a "flip car" and a "keep car." If your goal is pure profit, keep your tuning minimal. The return on investment for high-end performance parts is lower than the return on basic repairs. However, if you are building a personal vehicle to race your friends, maximizing performance is highly recommended.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

With over 100 unique vehicles available in Roblox Car Flipper, knowing which cars to keep, upgrade, or immediately sell is crucial for efficient progression. We have categorized the vehicle types based on their optimal role in your business model.

| Tier | Category Name | Primary Goal | Recommended Player Action |

S-Tier vehicles are rare finds that boast incredible base stats and extensive customization options. While they can be sold for astronomical prices, keeping at least one fully upgraded S-Tier car is essential if you plan to participate in community racing events. A-Tier cars, on the other hand, represent the sweet spot of the flipping economy—they have reasonable repair costs but command high demand from mid-game players looking for reliable daily drivers.
