<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Walkthrough">

For new players entering the garage for the first time, the initial hour of gameplay can feel overwhelming due to the variety of broken components and customization options. Following a structured progression path prevents resource depletion and establishes a stable stream of income.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Initial Tutorial">
    When you first launch the game, follow the guided tutorial strictly. This intro introduces you to the basic tools, shows you how to interact with damaged components, and walks you through your very first basic repair and sale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Source Your First Damaged Cars">
    After the tutorial, use your starting budget to purchase low-tier, highly damaged vehicles. These vehicles are cheap to acquire and serve as excellent practice projects for learning which parts damage most frequently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Systematic Repairs">
    Bring the vehicle to your repair bay. Always address structural and engine issues first. Removing rust, replacing worn engine blocks, and ensuring the chassis is sound are mandatory steps before any cosmetic work can begin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Basic Customization and Sell">
    Once the vehicle is mechanically sound, apply a clean paint job and basic visual upgrades to boost its appeal. Take the vehicle to the sales portal and list it to secure your first major profit margin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Prioritizing your tasks correctly prevents you from wasting expensive parts on a vehicle that still has a broken engine. Use the priority guide below to structure your workshop activities.

| Task Category | Primary Focus | Priority Level | Profit Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Structural Repair | Rust removal, chassis alignment | Critical | High |
| Engine Restoration | Cylinder replacement, fluid refills | Critical | High |
| Performance Tuning | Turbochargers, suspension upgrades | Medium | Moderate |
| Visual Customization | Custom paint, body kits, rims | Low | Moderate |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Customization Trap">
Do not spend your limited starting currency on expensive visual modifications or neon lights. A beautiful car with a broken engine sells for a fraction of its potential value. Focus entirely on mechanical repairs first.
</Callout>
