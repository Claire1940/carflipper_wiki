For this reason, high-end tuning should be reserved for rare vehicles or personal showcase builds that you intend to keep long-term.

For standard flips, stick to basic engine tunes, weight reduction, and suspension adjustments. These upgrades are relatively inexpensive but significantly improve the vehicle's overall rating, making it easier to attract high-paying buyers without eating into your profit margins.
