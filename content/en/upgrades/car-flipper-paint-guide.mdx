Applying a fresh coat of paint is a straightforward process, but doing it efficiently requires a structured approach. Following a consistent workflow prevents you from wasting valuable in-game currency on redundant paint jobs or applying premium finishes to low-value starter cars.

Use these steps to navigate the Tuning Shop's paint systems and achieve a flawless finish every time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Core Repairs">
    Before touching the spray booth, ensure all damaged parts are fully repaired. Structural integrity is the foundation of vehicle value, and paint should only be applied to a fully restored chassis.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter the Tuning Shop">
    Drive your repaired vehicle into the workshop's customization bay. This action opens the active customization menu, where you can access paint, body kits, and performance parts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Paint Finish and Color">
    Navigate to the paint menu and choose between primary and secondary color zones. Select a finish style (such as matte, metallic, or chrome) that matches the vehicle's design and tier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Apply">
    Review the total cost of the paint application. Confirm the purchase to permanently save the visual upgrades to your vehicle before preparing it for sale or collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
