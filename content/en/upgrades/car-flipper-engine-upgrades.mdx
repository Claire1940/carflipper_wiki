<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Engine Upgrade Workflow">

Successfully implementing **car flipper engine upgrades** requires a disciplined approach. Many new players make the mistake of buying expensive performance parts before fixing the basic mechanical failures of a damaged car. In Car Flipper, the sequence of operations determines your final profit efficiency.

Following a standardized workflow ensures that you don't waste resources on a vehicle that isn't structurally sound. The Tuning Shop should be your final stop before the car hits the showroom floor or the racing track.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire and Inspect Wrecks">
    Start by purchasing a damaged vehicle from the marketplace. Use your diagnostic tools to identify which engine components are missing or broken beyond repair.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Core Restoration">
    Before touching the performance settings, ensure the bodywork and basic engine functions are restored. A car must be "Functional" before it can be "Tuned."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Access the Tuning Shop">
    Drive your restored vehicle into the Tuning Shop area. This is where you can install performance parts like upgraded pistons, crankshafts, and nitro systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize for Purpose">
    Decide if the car is for racing or for flipping. If flipping, focus on parts that offer the highest "Value Add" per dollar spent. For racing, focus on top speed and handling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Performance Test">
    Take the car for a quick test drive or race against friends to ensure the engine upgrades are working correctly before listing it for sale.
  </Step>
</Steps>
