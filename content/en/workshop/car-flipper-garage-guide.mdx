The heart of the game lies in its five-step progression loop. Every vehicle you acquire goes through a strict preparation phase before it can be sold for a profit or added to your personal showcase. Skipping steps or over-investing in the wrong areas can severely impact your profit margins.

To maximize your efficiency, follow this step-by-step restoration workflow for every project vehicle that enters your garage:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Sourcing Damaged Vehicles">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mechanical Diagnostics and Repair">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bodywork and Structural Restoration">
    Repair dented panels, replace missing bumpers, and sand down rust patches. Ensuring the exterior is structurally sound is critical before moving to the paint booth.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visual Customization and Paint">
    Apply fresh paint coats, customize rims, and add spoilers. Use the game's hundreds of customization options to create a cohesive theme that appeals to collectors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Performance Tuning and Upgrades">
    Take the vehicle to the Tuning Shop. Install upgraded turbos, performance ECUs, and sport tires to increase top speed and handling stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="The Keep or Sell Decision">
    Evaluate the finished build. Sell the vehicle to secure immediate profit for workshop expansion, or keep it in your collection to show off or race against friends.
