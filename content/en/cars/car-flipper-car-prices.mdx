<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tuning Shop Upgrades and Price Modifiers">

Once a vehicle is fully repaired, taking it to the Tuning Shop is the best way to inflate the final sale price. Performance parts and visual customization options act as direct multipliers to the base restored value. By strategically upgrading specific systems, you can demand premium rates from buyers.

### Performance Upgrades Pricing Impact

Performance parts are highly recommended for cars you plan to keep or race, but they also add considerable value to resale builds. Investing in high-grade engine blocks and turbos yields the highest return on investment.

| Upgrade Category | Average Part Cost | Price Multiplier | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Engine Block | Medium - High | 1.35x base value | High-tier flips |
| Turbo Charger | High | 1.45x base value | Legendary cars |
| Sport Suspension | Low - Medium | 1.15x base value | Common & Uncommon |
| Racing Tires | Low | 1.10x base value | Quick turnarounds |

### Visual Customization Pricing Impact

Visual adjustments make your builds stand out and increase the buyer's valuation. While cosmetic upgrades do not boost performance stats, they are highly cost-effective for lower-tier vehicles.

| Cosmetic Option | Average Part Cost | Price Multiplier | Impact Level |
