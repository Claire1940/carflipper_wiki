Turning a profit in Car Flipper is a systematic process. You cannot simply buy a car and expect a windfall; you must follow the restoration loop to ensure every vehicle reaches its maximum potential before it hits the auction block.

  <Step num="1" title="Acquisition of Damaged Assets">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Structural and Mechanical Repair">
    Bring the vehicle to your workshop. Use your accumulated car parts to fix the engine, chassis, and internal components. A functional car is the baseline for all future value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Tuning Shop Customization">
    Visit the Tuning Shop to apply visual upgrades. Change the paint, wheels, and body kits. Personalization makes the vehicle stand out and significantly increases its "Cool Factor" and resale price.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Performance Upgrading">
    Install performance parts to boost speed, handling, and acceleration. Upgraded vehicles are highly sought after by players looking for competitive racing cars.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Sale or Collection">
    Decide whether to sell the car for immediate profit or add it to your collection. Selling funds your workshop expansion, while collecting increases your prestige and racing options.
  </Step>
