<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tuning Shop and Performance Parts Guide">

The Tuning Shop update introduces a deep layer of performance customization to Car Flipper. Once a vehicle is mechanically restored, you can install performance parts to alter its driving dynamics, top speed, and acceleration. Only install high-end performance parts on vehicles you intend to keep for racing or open-world exploration.
