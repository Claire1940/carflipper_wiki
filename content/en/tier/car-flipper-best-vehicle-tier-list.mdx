Every successful vehicle in the **car flipper best vehicle tier list** starts as a broken-down wreck. Skipping steps or prioritizing cosmetics over mechanical integrity will hurt your final valuation and slow down your workshop's progression.

To build a legendary fleet, you must master the transition from salvage to showcase. This requires a systematic approach to diagnostics, parts sourcing, and final customization.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Source a Damaged Vehicle">
    Visit the salvage yard or the digital marketplace to find cheap, broken-down vehicles. Look for cars with intact chassis but damaged engine components to get the best entry price.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute Core Mechanical Repairs">
    Strip down the damaged engine block and replace worn-out pistons, spark plugs, and radiators. A fully functional engine is the foundation of any high-value vehicle flip.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Restore the Exterior Body">
    Replace dented body panels, apply a fresh coat of paint, and install a clean set of wheels. Visual appeal directly influences the initial offers you receive from buyers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Tuning Shop Upgrades">
    Take the restored vehicle to the Tuning Shop to install performance parts. Upgrading the exhaust, suspension, and turbo systems will significantly boost the car's overall tier rating.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Make the Keep or Sell Decision">
    Evaluate the finished build. If it is an S-Tier masterpiece, add it to your personal collection. If it is an A-Tier profit maker, sell it immediately to fund your next project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
